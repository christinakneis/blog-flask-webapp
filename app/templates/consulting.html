Consulting & Custom Solutions

I offer consulting and custom solutions for system development, operations, and management. I use systems engineering principles to understand what you actually need, build the right thing, and make sure it works.

Quick Fix

A focused engagement to diagnose one problem and deliver a targeted solution.

Commitment: Hours
  • Focused session to understand your needs and identify the root issue
  • Clear action plan you can execute immediately
  • One targeted build: fix a broken process, automate a manual step, or unblock a bottleneck
Good fit when... you know something's off but need expert eyes to pinpoint it and fix it fast.

Level-Up

Optimize what you have or build something new that integrates with your existing systems.

Commitment: Weeks
  • Process review to find inefficiencies and opportunities
  • A polished landing page or single-page website
  • Lightweight internal tools that plug into your current stack
  • Workflow integrations that eliminate manual handoffs
Good fit when... your systems mostly work but need polish, or you want a new tool without rebuilding everything.

Big Build

Full-scale strategy and implementation - from roadmap to production.

Commitment: Months
  • Product or project roadmap with clear milestones
  • Full website or custom web application
  • Cloud infrastructure architecture (AWS) with security built in
  • System design that scales with your growth
Good fit when... you're building something new from the ground up or reimagining how your systems work together.

On Call

Ongoing partnership for continuous improvement and support.

Commitment: Ongoing
  • Monthly strategy check-ins to stay aligned with your goals
  • Reserved hours for advice, builds, or firefighting
  • Priority response when something breaks or priorities shift
  • Iterative improvements as your needs evolve
Good fit when... you want a trusted partner in your corner without the overhead of a full-time hire.

Not sure which fits? Feel free to reach out, tell me what's broken or what you wish worked better, and we'll figure out the right path together.
